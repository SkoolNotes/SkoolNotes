#+TITLE: Injectivity (math)
* In the context of Linear Algebra (Axler 3.15)
** #definition injective                                                :def:
   #+begin_quote
   A function $T : V \to W$ is called /injective/ if $Tu = Tv$ implies $u = v$
   #+end_quote
*** Properties
**** A map is injective iff it's null space equals $\{0\}$
