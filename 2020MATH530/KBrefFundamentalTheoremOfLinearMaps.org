* #definition Fundamental Theorem of Linear Maps                        :def:
  #+begin_quote
  Suppose $V$ is finite-dimensional and $T \in \mathcal L(V, W). Then \text{range }T is finite-dimensional and
  $$ \text{dim }V = \text{dim null }T + \text{dim range} T $$
  #+end_quote
