# Pains and feelings
**¿Como te sientes?**

## To Feel...
* Sentir (to feel) e=>ie
    * Siento 
* Doler (to hurt) — **notice! follows _gustar_ rules**
    * A mí, me duele _la_ muela ("to me, my teeth hurts me")
    * A mí, me duele _la_ cabeza ("to me, my heads hurts me")

## These things...

* Resfriado => cold
* Cansado => tired
* Pálido => pale
