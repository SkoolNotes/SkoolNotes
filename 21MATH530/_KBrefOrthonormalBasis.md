# Axler6.27 orthonormal basis [[def]{.smallcaps}]{.tag tag-name="def"} {#axler6.27-orthonormal-basis}

> An *orthonormal basis* of $V$ is an orthonormal list of vectors in $V$
> that is also a basis of $V$.

Pretty self explanatory.
