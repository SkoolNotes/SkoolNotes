* orthogonal decomposition
  An orthogonal decomposition is a way of writing some vector $v \neq 0 \in V$ as the scaled other vector $w \in V$ plus an orthogonal component
  #+begin_quote
  Suppose $u, v \in V$, with $v \neq 0$.
