# Axler6.56 Minimizing the distance to a subspace

> Suppose $U$ is a finite-dimenional subpsace of $V$, $v \in  V$, and
> $u \in  U$. Then, $$\begin{aligned}
> \lVert v - P_U v \rVert \leq  \lVert v - u \rVert
> \end{aligned}$$
