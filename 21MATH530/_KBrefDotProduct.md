# dot product [[def]{.smallcaps}]{.tag tag-name="def"} {#dot-product-1}

> For $x, y \in \RR ^n$, the *dot product* of $x$ and $y$, denoted
> $x \cdot y$, is defined by
>
> $$\begin{aligned}
> x\cdot y = x_1 y_1 + \cdots + x_n y_n
> \end{aligned}$$
>
> where $x= x_1, \ldots, x_n$, $y = y_1, \ldots, y_n$
