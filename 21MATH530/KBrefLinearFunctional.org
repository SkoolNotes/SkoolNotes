* Axler6.39 linear functional                                           :def:
  #+begin_quote
  A /linear functional/ on $V$ is a linear map from $V$ to $\mathbb F$. In other words, a linear functional is an element of $\mathcal L(V, \mathbb F)$
  #+end_quote
