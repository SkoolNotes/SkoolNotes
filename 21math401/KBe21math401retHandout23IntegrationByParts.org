#+TITLE: Integration By Parts
* $\int \ln x dx$

  \[\begin{aligned}
  \int \ln  x dx &=\int 1 \ln  x dx \\
  &= x \ln  x -\int x \frac{1}{x} dx \\
  &= x \ln  x -\int 1 dx \\
  &= \boxed{x \ln  x - x}
  \end{aligned}\]
* $\int\tan ^-x dx$

  \[\begin{aligned}
  \int\tan ^-x dx &= x \tan^- x -\int x \frac{1}{x^2+1} dx \\
  &= x\tan ^- x -\int \frac{du}{u}
  \end{aligned}\]
