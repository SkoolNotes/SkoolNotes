# area between curves

$$\begin{aligned}
  \int_{0}^{10} 2200e^{0.024t} dx - \int_0^{10} 1360e^{0.018t} dx &= \frac{1}{0.024} 2200e^{0.024t} - \frac{1}{0.018} 1360e^{0.018t}\\
  \implies &\quad \ \frac{1}{0.024} 2200e^{0.24} - \frac{1}{0.018} 1360e^{0.18} - \frac{1}{0.024} 2200 - \frac{1}{0.018} 1360
  \end{aligned}$$
