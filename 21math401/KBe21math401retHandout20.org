* cooling pizza
  Compute
  \[\begin{aligned}
  \int_{0}^{5} -110e^{-0.4t} dt
  \end{aligned}\]
  to the nearest degree.

  \[\begin{aligned}
  \int -110e^{-0.4t} dt = \frac{-110}{-0.4}e^{-0.4t} = 275e^{-0.4t}
  \end{aligned}\]

  Using the net change theorem,

  \[\begin{aligned}
  \int_{0}^{5} -110e^{-0.4t} dt
  \end{aligned}\]
