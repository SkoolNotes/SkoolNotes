# Problems on slide 38
## Problem 36
$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{x\to 0}\sqrt{x^3+x^2}sin\frac{\pi}{x}=0
\\& -1 \le sin\frac{\pi}{x} \le 1 \\&\therefore -\left| x \sqrt{x+1} \right| \le x\sqrt{x+1}sin\frac{\pi}{x}\lt \left| x \sqrt{x+1}\right|
\end{aligned}
$$
