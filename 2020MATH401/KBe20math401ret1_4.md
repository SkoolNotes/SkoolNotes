## 84
$$
\lim_{x\to 1}\frac{x^3+3x^2+5}{4-7x} = \frac{1+3+5}{4-7} = \frac{9}{-3} = \boxed{-3}
$$
## 85
$$
\lim_{x\to -2}\sqrt{x^2-6x+3} = \sqrt{4 - (-12) + 3} = \boxed{\sqrt{19}}
$$
