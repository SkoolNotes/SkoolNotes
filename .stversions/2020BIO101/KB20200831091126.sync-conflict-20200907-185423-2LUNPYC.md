# Scientific Writing
- No pronouns in scientific writing
- Don't say anything needs anything else
    - Need
    - Want
    - Like
    - Replace these things with
        - They do this
### Weak vs strong pullers of electrons
- Strong
    - Nitrogen
    - Oxygen
    - P
- Weak
    - Hydrogen
    - Carbon
