Linear combination aka elimination method
$$
\begin{align}
2x &+ 3y &= 5 \\
 x &+  y &= 1
\end{align}
$$
is equivalent to
$$
\begin{matrix}
2 &3 \\
1 &1
\end{matrix}
$$.
